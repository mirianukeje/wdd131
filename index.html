Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is located in the southwestern region of Nigeria along the Atlantic coast.The city is defined by it's dual nature: a dense urban core bordered by lagoons, creeks, and the Atlantic ocean. Lagos is not just a city, it is Nigeria's commercial heartbeat, where every corner hums with innovation, ambition, and possibilities. It is fast paced, demanding, and sometimes chaotic, but it is also where people learn to adapt, to rise, and to shine.
