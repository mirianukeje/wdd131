Mirian Ukeje - Site Plan

Site Name: William's Pet Home

I chose the name William's Pet Home to reflect a sense of care and belonging. 'William' adds a personal, heartfelt touch, while 'Pet Home' captures the vision of a safe, comforting space where every pet is treated like family.

Site Purpose

The purpose of William's Pet Home website is to provide pet owners and animal lovers with a trusted space to access reliable care, book grooming or boarding services, and adopt rescued stray animals in need of a loving home. This site connects users with a compassionate community dedicated to the well-being of pets, making it easier to care, connect, and give every animal a second chance at family.

Scenarios

Got questions? You're not alone. Here's what our visitors are asking and how our site makes it easy to find answers.

  • How do I book a weekend stay for my dog at William's Pet Home?
  • Are the rescued animals vaccinated before adoption?
  • Can I make a donation toward food and medical care for the strays?
  • What should I bring when dropping my pet off for boarding?
